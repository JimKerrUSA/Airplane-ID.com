# General Purpose Identification Apps

## Category Overview

General purpose identification apps attempt to identify anything pointed at by the camera. These "identify anything" apps use broad AI models rather than specialized single-category databases.

## AI Reliability Notes

From research findings:
- "Apps that use search engines like Google are more reliable than apps that use AI"
- "AI apps can give you more detailed information about any object"
- Hybrid approaches (AI + search) may be optimal

---

## UI/UX Patterns Observed

1. **Instant Camera View:** Opens directly to camera
2. **Tap to Identify:** Simple single-tap interaction
3. **Results Overlay:** Information appears over image
4. **Follow-up Questions:** Natural language queries
5. **History/Collection:** Past identifications saved
6. **Share Integration:** Send results to others
7. **Multi-modal:** Photo, voice, text input options

---

## Key Takeaways for Product Development

1. **Free options dominate** - Apple, Google, Microsoft all free
2. **Accessibility opportunity** - Seeing AI, Aipoly serve underserved market
3. **Specialization wins** - Category-specific apps often more accurate
4. **Voice integration** - CamFind's follow-up questions compelling
5. **Search engine backing** - Google/Apple more reliable than pure AI
6. **Built-in competition** - iOS features compete with third-party
