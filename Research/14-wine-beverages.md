# Wine & Beverage Identification Apps

## Category Overview

Wine identification apps scan labels to provide ratings, reviews, tasting notes, and purchasing options. These apps are popular with wine enthusiasts, sommeliers, and casual drinkers exploring new wines.

## Unique Features in Wine Apps

1. **Label Scanning:** Primary input method (not camera ID of wine itself)
2. **Tasting Notes:** Professional descriptions
3. **Food Pairings:** What to eat with wine
4. **Cellar Management:** Track wine inventory
5. **Drinking Windows:** When to open bottles
6. **Community Ratings:** Crowdsourced reviews
7. **Purchase Integration:** Buy identified wines

---

## UI/UX Patterns Observed

1. **Label Frame:** Guide for aligning bottle label
2. **Rating Stars/Numbers:** Community scores prominent
3. **Taste Profile Radar:** Visual flavor characteristics
4. **Price Range Display:** Value context
5. **Pairing Icons:** Food category suggestions
6. **Cellar Grid:** Wine collection management
7. **Tasting Notes Scroll:** Professional reviews

---

## Key Takeaways for Product Development

1. **Vivino dominates** - 70M users, clear market leader
2. **Label scanning, not bottle** - Apps scan text, not visual recognition
3. **Community is core** - User ratings drive value
4. **Cellar management valued** - Inventory tracking important
5. **Education component** - Learning to taste is feature
6. **Purchase integration** - E-commerce opportunity
7. **Delectable is free** - Unlimited free scanning exists
