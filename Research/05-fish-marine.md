# Fish & Marine Life Identification Apps

## Category Overview

Fish identification apps serve anglers, scuba divers, snorkelers, aquarium hobbyists, and marine biology enthusiasts. These apps often include fishing regulations and catch logging features.

## Unique Features in Fish Apps

1. **Fishing Regulations:** Legal size limits, seasons, bag limits
2. **Catch Logging:** Track catches with GPS and conditions
3. **Weather Data:** Tides, water temp, solunar
4. **Aquarium Mode:** For tank hobbyists
5. **Underwater Photos:** Works with snorkel/dive photos
6. **Regional Packs:** Location-specific species

---

## UI/UX Patterns Observed

1. **Catch Journal:** Log-style entries with photos
2. **Map Integration:** Where fish was found/caught
3. **Weather Widget:** Conditions at a glance
4. **Regulation Cards:** Legal info prominently displayed
5. **Size Reference:** Common length/weight info
6. **Habitat Icons:** Saltwater vs freshwater indicators

---

## Key Takeaways for Product Development

1. **Regulations are differentiator** - FishVerify stands out with compliance features
2. **Multiple use cases** - Fishing, diving, aquariums are distinct audiences
3. **Underwater photo challenges** - Low light, water distortion issues
4. **Regional accuracy varies** - Some species better covered than others
5. **Catch logging valued** - Personal record-keeping feature popular
6. **GPS integration expected** - Location tracking for catches
