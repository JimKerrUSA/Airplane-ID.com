# Document & Text Recognition (OCR) Apps

## Category Overview

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) apps use the camera to recognize and extract text from physical documents, signs, business cards, and other text-containing objects. Many include translation capabilities.

---

## Unique Features in OCR Apps

1. **Live Text (AR):** Real-time translation overlay
2. **Handwriting Recognition:** Cursive and print
3. **Text-to-Speech:** Read extracted text aloud
4. **Multi-language:** Recognize and translate simultaneously
5. **PDF Support:** Extract from documents
6. **Business Card Scanning:** Contact extraction
7. **Dictionary Integration:** Look up definitions

---

## Common Use Cases

1. **Travel:** Translate signs, menus, documents
2. **Business:** Digitize business cards
3. **Education:** Convert notes to digital text
4. **Accessibility:** Text-to-speech for vision impaired
5. **Research:** Extract quotes from books
6. **Document Management:** Digitize paper files

---

## UI/UX Patterns Observed

1. **Viewfinder Frame:** Guide for text alignment
2. **Language Selector:** Source and target language
3. **Copy Button:** One-tap text copying
4. **Share Sheet:** Export to other apps
5. **History/Recent:** Previously scanned text
6. **Real-time Overlay:** AR translation on camera
7. **Text Highlighting:** Show recognized regions

---

## Key Takeaways for Product Development

1. **iOS Live Text competes** - Built-in feature is very capable
2. **QuickScan proves free works** - Successful without monetization
3. **Translation is key feature** - Most valuable for travel
4. **Handwriting differentiates** - Still challenging for AI
5. **Speed matters** - Users expect instant results
6. **Privacy concerns** - On-device processing preferred
7. **AR overlay compelling** - Real-time translation highly valued
