# Vehicle Identification Apps

## Category Overview

Vehicle identification apps use AI to identify cars, planes, boats, and other vehicles from photos. This category spans automotive enthusiasts, aviation spotters, and general curious users.

## UI/UX Patterns Observed

### Car Apps
1. **Photo Grid Results:** Multiple possible matches displayed
2. **Spec Cards:** Key statistics prominently displayed
3. **Price Comparisons:** Market value information
4. **Collection Gallery:** Save identified vehicles
5. **Brand Organization:** Group by manufacturer

### Aviation Apps
1. **AR Overlay:** Camera view with flight info overlaid
2. **Map View:** Aircraft positions in real-time
3. **Flight Path Lines:** Visual trajectory display
4. **Aircraft Photos:** Actual photos of specific planes
5. **Radar Style:** Aviation-themed aesthetics

---

## Key Takeaways for Product Development

1. **AR is key for aviation** - Pointing at sky is compelling UX
2. **Car apps highly monetized** - Very limited free tiers
3. **Flightradar24 dominates** - 591K ratings, clear market leader
4. **Specs matter for cars** - Users want detailed information
5. **Historical data valued** - Flight playback is premium feature
6. **Multi-vehicle opportunity** - Few apps cover cars AND planes
